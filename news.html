Protesters mad at Tree Scaree Tails.

People have been protesting Tree Scaree Tails. I interviewed the CEO of MemeFlix for this. When I asked him if they were protesting because of the lack of quality of the movie, he responded no. He said that they were protesting since the movie was too scary, especially the last tail. After watching the movie, I can verify this. Savage Samuels beat up Loser Larrys

At a recent Loser Larry convention, a couple of teenage Savage Samuels walked up and beat up the Larrys. It was hiLarryious. Unsurprisingly, the police soon arrived and joined in with the Savage Samuels. The president himself arrived just to get the experience of beating up the losers with the savages. The losers were having a convention about how being a loser doesn't mean that you can't beat up other ranks. After this event, the losers moved one rank down, becoming even worse than the Certified Wimps.
